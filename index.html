About Us

Who We Are

Karabakh Company was established on July 27, 2025, as a high-standard virtual trucking organization. We focus on operational excellence in ETS2 and ATS, fostering a community built on discipline, structural integrity, and professional synergy.

We are not just a VTC; we are a family representing Azerbaijan on the global TruckersMP stage. Whether it's huge convoys or daily jobs, we drive with pride.

Our Rules

1. TruckersMP Guidelines
All drivers must strictly adhere to the official TruckersMP rules at all times. Bans may result in removal from the VTC.
2. Respect & Professionalism
Disrespect towards staff members or other drivers is not tolerated. We represent Karabakh Company with pride.
3. VTC Paint & Tag
During official convoys, you must use the official Karabakh Company paint job and player tag (Orange).
4. Convoy Etiquette
Keep distance, follow the Convoy Control instructions, and do not overtake unless necessary.
